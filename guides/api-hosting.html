Payment Process Explanation

Here's how the payment process works:

1

Select your payment method from the available options

Choose the payment option that works best for you. Options include Card, Mobile Money, and Bank Transfer. Each method is secure and processed through trusted payment gateways.

2

Enter your payment details securely

Fill in your payment information depending on your chosen method. All information is encrypted and kept safe using industry-standard security protocols. Your data never leaves our secure servers.

3

Receive instant confirmation of your payment

After submitting your payment, you will receive an immediate confirmation including transaction details, receipt number, and payment status. A confirmation email will also be sent to your registered email address.

4

Get setup credentials and support contact information

Once your payment is confirmed, you will receive login details, setup instructions, and support contact information to get started. Our team is available 24/7 for assistance.

Receive API Setup Instructions

After payment confirmation, you'll receive all the details you need to integrate and manage your API services.

API Endpoints

You will get the hosted API base URLs to connect your applications and send or receive data securely.

Authentication Keys

Receive API keys and access tokens to authenticate your apps and safely communicate with the backend.

Monitoring Dashboard

Access your dashboard to track API usage, monitor performance, and view analytics.

Technical Docs

Step-by-step technical documentation with full API reference, usage examples, and integration guidelines.
